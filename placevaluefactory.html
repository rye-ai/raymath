How to Play

  1. 1 Get your number order card.
  2. 2 Banker counts out the single blocks (ones).
  3. 3 Check the "Big Rule"! If you have more than 9, make a bundle of 10.
  4. 4 Call the Delivery Driver!
  5. 5 Driver takes the bundle to the Builder.
  6. 6 Builder makes the trade: 10 ones for 1 ten rod.
  7. 7 Driver returns the ten rod to the Banker.
  8. 8 Still more than 9 ones left? Go back to step 3!
  9. 9 Job Done! when the Banker has fewer than 10 ones.
